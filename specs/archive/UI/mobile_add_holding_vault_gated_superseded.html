Vault — coming with multisig support

A Vault holds Bitcoin under multiple keys — two or more devices, often held by different people or in different places, all required to spend. It's the long-term, ceremonial-spend tier.

Multisig support ships in an upcoming iteration. To track Bitcoin held on a single hardware wallet today, set up a Strongbox — when multisig arrives, your Strongbox can be promoted to a Vault.
