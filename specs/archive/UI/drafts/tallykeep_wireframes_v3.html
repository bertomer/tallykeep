Add your first Holding

A Holding is a purpose-bound bucket of value. Pick the type that matches who actually holds the keys.
Account
Custodial
Funds at a custodial provider (Kraken, Bitstamp, Swissquote). They hold the keys; you grant API access to read balance and trigger withdrawals to a whitelisted address.
Purse
User-keys, online
Wallet on a connected day-to-day device. You hold the keys; signing is light. Daily-spending tier of security.
Strongbox
User-keys, offline
Wallet on a hardware or airgapped signer. You hold the keys; signing requires the external device. Cold reserve.
Vault
Ceremonial access
Multisig, timelocks, geographic separation, inheritance setup. Designed for long-term holdings that move rarely. v1 stores Vault metadata but only single-key descriptors are accepted (multisig support v2).
2 · Add first Holding (type picker)
The four types as four equal cards, with explicit descriptions of who holds the keys. Vault has its v1 limitation called out honestly — that's the "honest abstraction" principle in action. On Beginner profile, Strongbox and Vault would be hidden.
