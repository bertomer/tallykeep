Privacy notice — read this
Because we run the backend, we see things you'd otherwise keep on your machine: your wallet descriptors (xpubs), the addresses TallyKeep watches, your transaction labels and categorizations, and your balance over time.

We never see your Bitcoin keys, signing material, or anything that lets us spend your funds. That stays on your hardware wallet (or your phone, for Lightning). Our threat model is in the docs — read it before you commit money.
