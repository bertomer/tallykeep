How TallyKeep works

  • Open source
    The code is public. Anyone can read it.
  • No accounts
    We don't know who you are. No email, no profile, no record on our side.
  • Your keys stay yours
    They live on this device, on your hardware wallet, or with your custodial provider — never on our servers. If you lose them, we cannot recover them.
TallyKeep is a tool, not a service. You hold the funds. You hold the keys. You're the one responsible for backing them up and keeping them safe — we can't do that for you and we can't recover what's lost. Tap below to confirm you've read this and you accept it.
